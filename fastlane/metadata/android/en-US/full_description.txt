Rivu Gallery is a simple, private gallery for browsing photos and videos on Android.

It focuses on the essentials: albums, collection view, videos, favorites, recycle bin support, image properties, EXIF tools, basic editing, and customizable display options.

Key features:

- Browse local photos and videos
- Organize media by albums and folders
- View favorites and videos from the bottom navigation
- Recover deleted media from the recycle bin
- View image properties and metadata
- Remove EXIF metadata when needed
- Customize themes, layout, sorting, grouping, and visibility options

Rivu Gallery is open source and based on Fossify Gallery, licensed under GPLv3.
